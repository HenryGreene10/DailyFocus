Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Daily Focus ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

Daily Focus is designed to work offline and does not require an account. We collect minimal information:

  • Push notification token: If you grant permission, your device's push notification token is used solely to send you daily reminders to complete your story. This token is not linked to any personal identity and is not shared with third parties.
  • Local app data: Your focus streak and reading session data are stored entirely on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics tools or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
  • We do not store any user data on external servers.

3. Push Notifications

Daily Focus may send you push notifications to remind you to complete your daily story. You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone's Settings app under Notifications → Daily Focus. Disabling notifications does not affect your ability to use the app.

4. Children's Privacy

Daily Focus is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@dailyfocusapp.com
